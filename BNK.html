BNK48
               BNK48 (in short for Bangkok48) is a Thai Idol Group and the 2nd international sister group of AKB48, after Indonesia's JKT48. The group has their own theater in The Mall Bangkapi, on Bangkok, Thailand. BNK48's logo features the orchid color (bright rich purple color) a popular flower in Thailand.
